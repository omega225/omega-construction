What We Build

From the foundation to the finishing touches, Omega Construction delivers full-service residential and commercial construction across Virginia.

Service 01

Custom Home Construction

Your home is the most personal project you'll ever undertake, and we treat it that way. We work closely with you from the initial design consultation through the final walkthrough to ensure every detail reflects your vision, your lifestyle, and your budget.

Our team of experienced builders brings decades of residential construction expertise to every custom home we build, and we manage every trade in-house to maintain quality control at every stage of the process.

  • Ground-up builds on existing lots or land you purchase
  • Full design-build services and architect coordination
  • Energy-efficient construction and smart home integration
  • Detailed cost estimates with no hidden fees or surprises

Service 02

Renovation & Remodeling

Transforming an existing space requires just as much skill and planning as building from scratch — sometimes more. We specialize in renovations that honor the character of your existing home while delivering modern functionality and finishes that feel completely new.

From a single room refresh to a whole-house renovation, our crews minimize disruption to your daily life and maintain clean, organized job sites throughout every phase of the project.

  • Kitchen and bathroom remodels with full design coordination
  • Basement finishing and addition construction
  • Structural modifications, room conversions, and open-plan layouts
  • Historic and period-appropriate renovation work

Service 03

Commercial Construction

Commercial projects demand a higher level of coordination, compliance expertise, and schedule discipline than residential work. We bring the same craft and commitment to every commercial build while meeting the additional regulatory and operational requirements that come with the territory.

Our commercial division handles everything from retail fit-outs and office renovations to ground-up mixed-use developments, always with an eye on your timeline and your bottom line.

  • Office, retail, and medical facility build-outs and renovations
  • ADA compliance and commercial code expertise
  • Phased construction to minimize business disruption
  • Ground-up commercial builds and mixed-use developments

Service 04

Project Management & Consulting

Not every client needs Omega to swing the hammers — some just need a trusted expert in their corner to make sure the right work gets done the right way. Our consulting services provide professional oversight, planning, and accountability for projects where you're sourcing your own trades or managing the build yourself.

Whether you need a pre-purchase inspection, a second opinion on a contractor's estimate, or ongoing construction management support, we bring our full expertise to bear on your behalf without the full general contractor markup.

  • Owner's representative services and third-party oversight
  • Contractor vetting, bid review, and contract negotiation
  • Schedule and budget monitoring with weekly reporting
  • Pre-purchase property assessments and construction feasibility studies

Our Approach

How We Work

1

Discovery Call

We start with a free 30-minute phone or video call to understand your project goals, timeline, and budget so we can determine how Omega can best help.

2

Site Assessment

We visit the property in person to evaluate existing conditions, identify any potential challenges, and gather the information needed to prepare an accurate, detailed estimate.

3

Detailed Estimate

You receive a comprehensive, line-item estimate covering all materials, labor, and contingencies — no vague allowances or hidden costs that inflate later.

4

Build Phase

Our crews get to work according to an agreed-upon schedule, with your dedicated project manager providing regular updates and handling any decisions that arise along the way.

5

Final Walkthrough

We walk through the finished space together to verify every item on our punch list is complete and that you are fully satisfied before we consider the project closed.

Our primary service area is Northern Virginia — including Fairfax County, Arlington, Loudoun, Prince William, and the City of Alexandria. We also take on select projects in the broader DC metro area and the Shenandoah Valley corridor. If your project is outside these areas, contact us and we'll let you know whether we can accommodate it.

Timelines vary significantly by project scope. A kitchen or bathroom remodel typically runs 4–8 weeks once materials are on site. A whole-home renovation or addition can take 4–8 months depending on complexity. A ground-up custom home generally requires 10–16 months from permit issuance to final walkthrough. During our Site Assessment and Detailed Estimate phases, we'll provide a project-specific schedule you can plan around.

We work on a draw schedule tied to project milestones rather than charging large lump sums upfront. A typical structure involves a modest mobilization deposit to secure your start date, followed by progress payments at key build milestones (e.g., framing complete, rough-ins approved, drywall finished), and a final payment upon your signed acceptance at the walkthrough. Exact terms are documented in your contract before any work begins.
