Who We Are

Our Story

Built on Integrity,
Driven by Craft

We started Omega Construction over two decades ago with a single guiding belief: that good work speaks for itself. What began as a small residential crew in Northern Virginia has grown into a full-service general contracting firm handling everything from custom home builds to large-scale commercial renovations — and we're still guided by those same founding values every day.

Over the years we've had the privilege of working on hundreds of projects across Virginia, and each one has reinforced something we already knew — our clients aren't just hiring a contractor, they're trusting us with their homes, their businesses, and their livelihoods. That's a responsibility we take seriously, and it shows in how we plan, communicate, and execute on every job we take.

We're proud of where we've been, and we're excited about where we're headed. Whether you're building your dream home from the ground up or giving an existing space a second life, we'd love the opportunity to bring that vision to life alongside you.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

Integrity

We do what we say and say what we mean — every promise we make is a commitment we keep.

The People Behind the Work

Meet the Team

Robert Calloway

Founder & Principal

Robert founded Omega Construction in 1999 after fifteen years learning the trade from the ground up across Northern Virginia. He oversees every project personally, ensuring that Omega's standards of quality and integrity are upheld from the first consultation to the final walkthrough.

Maria Torres

Project Manager

Maria brings over a decade of project management experience to every Omega build, keeping timelines on track and clients informed every step of the way. Her background in architecture and construction management means she speaks the language of both builders and clients fluently.

Derek Harris

Site Superintendent

Derek has been managing jobsites for Omega since 2008, bringing an unwavering eye for detail and a reputation for running the cleanest, most organized sites in the business. Subcontractors and crew members alike describe him as the kind of superintendent who raises everyone's game just by showing up.
